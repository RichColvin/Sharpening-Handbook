KenJigs
Note: The HanJig was designed for use with the KVM knife jigs. It is not as good a jig for use with the KJ knife jigs.

The Tormek KS-123 is a better approach to use for the KJ knife jigs.

The KenJig was one of the first add-on jigs made for the Tormek, and one of the easiest to make, and easiest to use.

It is based on the Dutchman's guide for Grinding Angle Adjustment for the Tormek, and named after the Tormek forum's fearless leader, Ken Schroeder.
