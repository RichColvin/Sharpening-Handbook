Dutchman's Guide

Ton Nillesen (also known as "the Dutchman") put together a guide for setting grinding angles on knives when using the SVM-45, the SVM-100 (no longer made, but still quite usable), or the SVM-140 grinding jigs.

His method is a very simple way for setting the machine up to grind specific angle you desire. It uses tables to setup the jigs based on the grind wheel size, allowing you adjust at the wheel size diminishes with use.

It is published in these in A5-format. Whilst they say they are for the T-7, these equally apply to the T-2000, T-3, T-4, and T-8:

  • Grinding angle adjustment A5 serial.pdf, and
  • More math for the Tormek grinder A5 serial.pdf.

Ton also published his spreadsheets, which can be opened in Excel or OpenOffice:

  • 1 Grinding angle adjustment table.ods,
  • 2 USB to stone distance table.ods, and
  • USB adjustment table.ods.
