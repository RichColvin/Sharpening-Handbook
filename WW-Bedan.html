Bedan

  

Edge Angle

You will find something more in woods than in books. Trees and stones will teach you that which you can never learn from masters.

Saint Berna

As the points of the Bedan are used when this tool is employed, these must be the focus of the sharpening activity.

When honing, I use a 1,000 grit diamond stone to touch up the left & right sides, and the bottom.

Other Opinions

  • Ernie Conover recommends an α of 25°-30° in The Lathe Book.
  • Marty Kiminsky noted that an α of 15-30° is generally recommended for this tool in "Woodturning" magazine, issue # 352. (He did not note a personal preference though.)
