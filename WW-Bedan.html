Bedan

As the points of the Bedan are used when this tool is employed, these must be the focus of the sharpening activity.

When honing, I use a 1,000 grit diamond stone to hone the left & right sides, and the bottom.

Notes & Comments

Information regarding Grindstones

Ernie Conover recommends an α of 25°-30° in The Lathe Book.

Marty Kiminsky noted that an α of 15-30° is generally recommended for this tool. (He did not note a personal preference though.) See also, "Woodturning" magazine, issue # 352.
