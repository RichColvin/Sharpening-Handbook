Grooving tool

The cutters are sharpened to have a negative rake on the top of the tool.

The tool is then honed by rubbing a 150+ grit diamond file against the top of the tool. The area to be honed is shown in the pictures as the area marked red.

Allan Batty's video (link below) is an excellent reference on how to sharpen these tools, and his guidance on how he modifies them to make them work better.

Woodworkers are knotty.
