General Guidelines
Point Angle Clearance Angle Notes
130° 0° Grind the bit so that it has a 2-facet grind (the primary facet). No secondary facet is needed, and would probably weaken the bit's insert.

The Tormek DBS-22 Drill Bit Sharpening Attachment can be used to sharpen this drill.
