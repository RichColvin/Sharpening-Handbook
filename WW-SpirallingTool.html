Spiralling & Texturing Tool

The Spiralling Tool wheel (shown to the right) is sharpened by rubbing a 120 - 150 grit diamond file against the bevel on the wheel whist it is being spun. The surface to be sharpened is indicated by the arrow.

The wheel can be spun by hand, or by pressing it against a spinning piece of scrap wood.

The Texturing Tool wheel (shown to the left) should be spun by pressing it against a spinning piece of scrap wood (400-500 RPM).

This is highly recommended as the edges are pretty sharp points !

About this site
Remember : The goal of sharpening is to produce sharp tools, and these tools can injure you if mishandled. Safety measures should be followed to protect yourself and those in your shop. Be sure to read and follow all instructions from the manufacturer, and and utilize proper safety equipment.
